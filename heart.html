PressHeartToContinue

PressHeartToContinue is a delightful channel created by Brooke "Dodger" Leigh Lawson. She is a vlogger and a "let's player" that does videos about gaming news and of course very fun to watch let's plays.

The video you see here, is her playthrough of "SHERLOCK HOLMES: CRIMES AND PUNISHMENTS". Her personality is infectious and she is a joy to watch. Intelligent and knowledgeable on all things gaming, you would do yourself a disservice if you did not also keep up with her regular gaming news videos.

Don't forget to visit her channel PressHeartToContinue and be sure to add her to your subscription list.
